My Role In This Process

In 2023 I took over the micromilling for the Quantitative Fisheries Lab. I work the Pelagic Fisheries Lab to select the samples we want to run stable isotope analysis on. This includes cleaning the data, and balancing the number of samples selected across different variables. Once I receive the mounted otoliths from the Pelagic Fisheries Lab, I run each sample on the micromill. The micromill is a wonderful, but tempermental, machine. My job also includes maintaining the micromill, as well as troubleshooting any issues that arise.

Once the milling is completed, I prep the samples to be sent out for analysis.

In this specific position, I was tasked with writing a formal protocol for the sample selection workflow and operating the micromill for the bluefin tuna otolith work.
